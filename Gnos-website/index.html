A Private Study, Not a Cloud.

Gnos is built on the principle of Digital Sovereignty. Your research library, your ink sketches, and your private notes exist only on your device. We cannot see them, and we cannot track them. It is your space, entirely offline and under your control.

THE INFINITE CANVAS

Think in Ink.

Our integrated sketch engine isn't an afterthought—it's the heart of the experience. Use the canvas to map out complex ideas, annotate PDFs, or bridge the gap between your reading and your writing.

  • Cross-Format: Import EPUB, PDF, TXT, or MD and start sketching immediately.
