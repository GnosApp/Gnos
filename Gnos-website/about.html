The Intent.

A note from the developer of Gnos.

I believe that the tools we use to think should be as quiet and respectful as a library. Gnos was built because I wanted a single space to house my entire intellectual life—my PDFs, my field notes, and my audio lectures—without the distraction of "the cloud" or the prying eyes of data harvesters.

The project is currently in early development. I am spending my time polishing the interaction between the canvas and the reader, ensuring that when you draw a line on a page, it feels like graphite on paper.
